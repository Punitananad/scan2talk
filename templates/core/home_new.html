Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about Scan2Talk and how it protects your privacy.

How does Scan2Talk protect my privacy?

Your phone number is never displayed on the QR code. When someone scans it, they can send you a message through our secure platform, and you'll receive it via your preferred channel (SMS, WhatsApp, or email) without revealing your actual number.

Is there a mobile app required?

No app required! Anyone can scan your QR code using their phone's camera. You'll receive notifications via SMS, WhatsApp, or email—whatever you prefer. It's that simple!

How long does the QR code tag last?

Our premium QR code tags are built to last 3+ years. They're waterproof, UV-resistant, and designed to withstand all weather conditions. The adhesive is strong enough to stay in place but can be removed without leaving residue.

Can I use it for multiple vehicles?

Absolutely! You can manage multiple vehicles from a single dashboard. Each vehicle gets its own unique QR code, and you can track all interactions in one place. Perfect for families or businesses with multiple vehicles.

What if someone misuses the QR code?

You have full control! You can block specific users, disable your QR code temporarily, or deactivate it completely from your dashboard. We also have rate limiting and spam protection built in to prevent abuse.

How much does it cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from just ₹299 for a single QR code tag with lifetime validity. No hidden fees, no subscriptions. You can also choose prepaid or pay-per-use options based on your needs.
