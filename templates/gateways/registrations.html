About Deregistration

What happens when you deregister a QR code?

  • The gateway is marked as inactive (is_active = False)
  • The QR code status changes back to "Available"
  • The vehicle number becomes available for new registrations
  • The QR code can be activated again by another user
  • All historical data (access count, interactions) is preserved

Use cases:

  • User requests to remove their registration
  • Vehicle sold or transferred to new owner
  • Duplicate or incorrect registrations
  • Testing or administrative cleanup
